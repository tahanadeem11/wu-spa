What to Expect

Your facial journey at Wu Spa begins with a thorough skin analysis. Our experienced estheticians in Charlotte NC and Newell, NC will examine your skin type, identify specific concerns, and discuss your skincare goals. This consultation ensures your facial treatment is perfectly customized to address your unique needs.

During your facial session, you'll experience a multi-step process designed to cleanse, exfoliate, extract, and nourish your skin. We begin with a gentle cleansing to remove makeup and impurities, followed by exfoliation to slough away dead skin cells. Our skilled estheticians perform extractions with precision and care, removing blackheads and unclogging pores. A customized mask is then applied to address your specific skin concerns, whether it's hydration, brightening, or anti-aging. The treatment concludes with a luxurious massage, serum application, and moisturizer to lock in hydration.

Each facial treatment typically lasts 50-60 minutes, allowing ample time for deep relaxation and comprehensive skin care. After your session, your skin will feel refreshed, rejuvenated, and radiant. Our estheticians will provide personalized recommendations for at-home skincare to maintain and enhance your results.
