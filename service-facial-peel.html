Facial Peel Treatment

Transform your skin with our advanced facial peel treatments at Wu Spa. Our expert estheticians in Charlotte NC and Newell, NC use professional-grade chemical peels to remove dead skin cells, reveal smoother texture, and address a variety of skin concerns. Whether you're dealing with fine lines, uneven skin tone, acne scars, or sun damage, our customized facial peel treatments can help restore your skin's natural radiance and youthfulness.

Peel Types

  • Glycolic Acid Peel: Alpha-hydroxy acid peel that exfoliates the skin's surface, reducing fine lines, improving texture, and brightening the complexion. Perfect for all skin types.

  • Salicylic Acid Peel: Beta-hydroxy acid peel ideal for oily and acne-prone skin. Penetrates deep into pores to unclog and reduce breakouts.

  • Lactic Acid Peel: Gentle exfoliation suitable for sensitive skin. Hydrates while removing dead skin cells, leaving skin soft and radiant.

  • Enzyme Peel: Natural fruit enzyme treatment that gently dissolves dead skin cells without irritation. Perfect for sensitive or reactive skin.

  • Retinol Peel: Advanced treatment that combines exfoliation with anti-aging benefits, stimulating collagen production and reducing signs of aging.

Benefits of Facial Peel

  • Removes dead skin cells and unclogs pores

  • Reduces fine lines and wrinkles

  • Improves skin texture and smoothness

  • Fades age spots and hyperpigmentation

  • Minimizes acne scars and blemishes

  • Stimulates collagen production

  • Brightens and evens skin tone

  • Reduces pore size appearance

  • Enhances product absorption

What to Expect

Your facial peel journey begins with a comprehensive skin consultation. Our experienced estheticians in Charlotte NC and Newell, NC will assess your skin type, discuss your concerns, and determine the most appropriate peel type and strength for your needs. This personalized approach ensures optimal results while minimizing any potential side effects.

During your facial peel treatment, your skin will be thoroughly cleansed and prepared. The chemical solution is then carefully applied to your face, where it works to break down the bonds between dead skin cells. You may experience a mild tingling or warming sensation, which is normal and indicates the peel is working. The solution is left on for a specific duration based on your skin's tolerance and the peel's strength, then neutralized and removed.

After your treatment, your skin may appear slightly red or feel tight, which typically subsides within a few hours. Over the next few days, you'll notice your skin beginning to peel or flake, revealing the fresh, new skin underneath. This process is natural and indicates the treatment is working effectively. Our estheticians will provide detailed aftercare instructions to ensure optimal healing and results.
