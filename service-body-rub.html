What to Expect

Your body rub session at Wu Spa begins with a consultation to understand your specific needs and concerns. Our experienced therapists in Charlotte NC and Newell, NC will discuss your muscle tension, stress levels, pain points, and any areas requiring special attention. This personalized approach ensures your body rub is tailored to address your unique physical condition and wellness goals.

During your body rub session, you'll experience therapeutic techniques applied to your entire body, focusing on areas of tension and discomfort. Our therapists use a combination of long, flowing strokes, kneading, and targeted pressure to release muscle tension, improve circulation, and promote deep relaxation. The treatment may incorporate premium massage oils or lotions to enhance glide and provide additional therapeutic benefits. We work systematically through your body, addressing each area with care and precision.

A typical body rub session lasts 60-90 minutes, allowing comprehensive coverage of your entire body. You'll feel immediate relief as tension releases and circulation improves. After your session, you'll experience reduced muscle stiffness, improved flexibility, and a deep sense of relaxation. Many clients report better sleep, reduced pain, and increased energy levels following their body rub treatment.
