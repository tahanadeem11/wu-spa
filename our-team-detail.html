Wumi

Founder, Wu Spa

With a foundation rooted in both science and soul, I bring a versatile background shaped by years of experience in five-star luxury hotels, elite wellness boutiques, and holistic skincare studios.

As a graduate of California State University with a Bachelor's in Sociology and Marketing, and a former Project Manager, I blend strategic precision with intuitive care. My journey began in the U.S. Navy as a Hospital Corpsman, where I learned the profound connection between healing and human resilience. Today, that same discipline and empathy infuse every treatment I create — each massage, each facial — a world-class experience designed to restore balance, serenity, and renewal.

Wu Spa is more than a place of beauty; it's a sanctuary where peace becomes practice and service becomes art.
