What to Expect

Your skin care journey at Wu Spa begins with a comprehensive skin consultation. Our expert estheticians in Charlotte NC and Newell, NC will analyze your skin type, assess your specific concerns, and discuss your skincare goals. This detailed evaluation helps us create a personalized treatment plan and product regimen tailored to your unique skin needs. We'll examine factors such as skin texture, tone, hydration levels, and any specific issues like acne, hyperpigmentation, or signs of aging.

During your skin care treatment, you'll experience a series of professional techniques designed to address your specific concerns. Our estheticians use premium skincare products and advanced treatment methods including deep cleansing, exfoliation, extraction, masks, and specialized serums. Each treatment is customized to your skin type and concerns, ensuring optimal results. A typical skin care session lasts 60-90 minutes, providing comprehensive care for your skin. You'll notice immediate improvements in skin texture, radiance, and overall appearance. After your treatment, your esthetician will provide personalized recommendations for at-home care to maintain and enhance your results. Regular skin care treatments help maintain healthy, glowing skin and address concerns before they become more significant issues.
