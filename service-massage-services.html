Massage Services

Experience the transformative power of professional massage therapy at Wu Spa. What We Offer

  • Swedish Massage: Gentle, flowing strokes perfect for relaxation and stress relief. Ideal for first-time clients and those seeking a calming experience.

  • Deep Tissue Massage: Targeted pressure to release chronic muscle tension and alleviate pain. Excellent for athletes and individuals with persistent discomfort.

  • Hot Stone Massage: Heated smooth stones combined with massage techniques to deeply relax muscles and improve circulation.

  • Prenatal Massage: Specially designed for expecting mothers to relieve pregnancy-related discomfort and promote relaxation.

  • Sports Massage: Therapeutic massage focused on preventing and treating athletic injuries while enhancing performance.

What to Expect

When you arrive at Wu Spa for your massage service in Charlotte NC or Newell, NC, our experienced massage therapists will begin with a consultation to understand your specific needs, any areas of concern, and your preferred pressure level. You'll be provided with a comfortable, private treatment room where you can relax and unwind.

During your session, our skilled therapists use premium massage oils and techniques tailored to your preferences. Whether you choose Swedish massage for gentle relaxation or deep tissue massage for targeted relief, each treatment is designed to address your unique wellness goals. Sessions typically range from 60 to 90 minutes, allowing ample time for complete relaxation and therapeutic benefit.

After your massage, you'll feel deeply relaxed and rejuvenated. Our therapists may provide recommendations for stretching exercises or follow-up sessions to maintain the benefits of your treatment.
